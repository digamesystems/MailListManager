As a stormwater management professional, I thought you might be interested in a new tool my company is developing to help engineers like yourself keep track of QPEs driving inspection requirements. We call it, QPE Assistant.

A little background:

My wife is a Civil Engineer who is also a QSD and QSP. We were talking about the new 2022 General Construction Permit requirements and she mentioned the steps around predicting QPEs for her construction sites were rather tedious.

- We have nerdy conversations. :)

When she mentioned that her firm had to make daily visits to the National Weather Service web site for each of their construction sites and perform a set of manual calculations, I decided to work with my team to make a little web appliction that automates this process.

So, we built QPE Assistant.

QPE Assistant allows you to create and maintain your own list of sites, fine tune their GPS loctions and run reports based on real-time data from the National Weather Service to predict upcoming QPEs for each of them over the next seven days.
