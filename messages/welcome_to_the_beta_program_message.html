I'm John Price, Co-Creator of QPE Assistant, and I'm thrilled to welcome you to the QPE Assistant Beta Program!

Now that you're all signed up, take a moment to explore our app. It's pretty straightforward.

Start by creating a couple of sites - just use the approximate address as a starting point and then refine your locations using the cursor to specify the exact spot. Once saved, you can run a QPE report, which will give you a rundown of all your sites using real-time data from the National Weather Service.
